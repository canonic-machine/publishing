inherits: /

## Content Concepts

### artifact identifier

A stable identifier for a published artifact (e.g., arXiv ID).

---

### dissemination

The act of distributing evidence-bearing artifacts without changing them.

---

### elapsed time

The duration between two timestamps.

---

### evidence window

The declared time span and commits used for reconstruction.

---

### freeze tag

A ledger tag that anchors a freeze declaration.

---

### freeze timestamp

The time recorded for the freeze declaration.

---

### manuscript

The narrative paper produced under PAPER.

---

### publication

A public release of a manuscript and its evidence references.

---

### publication timestamp

The time a publication artifact is released.

---

### publishing

The scope that packages and releases PAPER artifacts.

---

### submission

A recorded act of submitting a manuscript to a venue.

---

### time-to-publication

The elapsed time between freeze and publication.

---

### traceability

The property of being linked to ledger evidence.

---
