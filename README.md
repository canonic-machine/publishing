# PUBLISHING

Dissemination of evidence-bearing artifacts with freeze anchoring and time-to-publication disclosure.

## Purpose

PUBLISHING exists to disseminate evidence-bearing artifacts. It anchors each published artifact to its freeze tag and evidence window, discloses time-to-publication metrics, and maintains minimal governance without modifying or reinterpreting evidence.

---

## In Scope

- Dissemination of evidence-bearing artifacts
- Freeze tag and evidence window anchoring
- Time-to-publication disclosure (freeze timestamp, publication timestamp, elapsed time)
- Minimal publication governance

## Out of Scope

- Modification or reinterpretation of evidence
- Introduction of new governance or enforcement
- Interpretation and narrative (governed by PAPER)
